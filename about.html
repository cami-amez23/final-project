Who Am I?

I am a bilingual graphic design student at Fresno State
University, California. I have a passion for both the
traditional and digital arts.I love experimenting with
different mediums and am constantly learning and applying
new methods of artistry. I have a deep understanding of
most digital design softwares such as Adobe Photoshop,
Illustrator, and Animate. I am well-versed in large scale
murals,screen printing, letterpress, collage, and some
apparel design. Collarborating in group projects and
community art projects are always fun for me! Please
feel free to contact me for any upcoming projects or
inquiries. I would love to work with you!
